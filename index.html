I am a passionate software engineer who enjoys developing responsive websites that incorporate elements of modern design using technologies like HTML, CSS3, Grids, and JavaScript. Additionally, I enjoy testing backend routes, problem-solving, and reading errors to find the source of a bug. I have experience working with JavaScript, Python, React, Express, Flask, Node.js, and many more technologies. I love the process of developing my ideas into existence and finding ways to research and implement new technologies to improve user experience.

  • The Beginning

    Growing up, I enjoyed learning about computers interacting with software, and following software updates to explore new features and improvements. At an early age, I taught myself how to manipulate the operating system on my iPhone to customize the user interface.

  • 2019-2021

    Expanding my knowledge

    In college, my interest in technology grew and I earned an IT certificate while self-teaching myself to code. I enjoyed researching documentation and learning new technologies. Programming allowed me to merge my two interests of art and tech to develop my ideas into existence.

  • 2017-2021

    Professional Growth

    While working for the County of Sonoma as a Human Services Supervisor, I developed my leadership and collaboration skills. I was a subject matter expert and assisted with the planning and development of the first California Statewide Automated Welfare System in which I worked with 58 countries across the state. Being amongst Software Engineers inspired me, and this further developed my passion for engineering. I knew that I wanted to become more involved in creating and developing applications. This led me to change carriers.

  • August 2021

    Following my passion

    I joined a 24-week immersive web development program where I shared my skills in JavaScript, Python, along with other technologies. I applied my knowledge to build four dynamic full-stack applications.
